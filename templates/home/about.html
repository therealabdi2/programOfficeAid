    A leap towards digitalization

Web Portals are not new in Universities, but they always have space for more features. The workflow of each tool is quite dependent according to the oneâ€™s need. With IIUI, each year thousands of students go through the process of submitting forms, getting answers to queries, but due to the lack of a suitable system, the university is forced to use paperwork.

This web application, as the name suggests will aid Program Office Staff and Students. The web app can be used by authorized Program Office Staff to review different kinds of forms as well as verify all the documents and make announcements; all online and on the go, whereas students can enroll in courses or drop courses etc. and It can also be used to monitor student queries and their feedback.

Every step taken towards digitalization over paperwork is a step towards future development. With the use of this portal, management and records handling will be just one click away.

Program Office Aid is specifically designed for International Islamic University, Islamabad, and aims for less paperwork and goals to move more towards digitalization.

This web application can help by automating half the process and remove chances of human error by implementing proper authentication of form before the student can submit. It can also be used to monitor student queries and their feedback on staff. Moreover, an announcements panel can be used as one centralized location to update students
